The Journey Begins

Every great story has a beginning, here's mine

Finding My Passion

My journey in leadership started with a simple goal to make a difference. Joining PADC was not just about being in an organization, but about building connections and working toward a bigger purpose. Bringing people together and organizing meaningful events gave me a strong sense of fulfillment.

Leadership Through Action

As I took on different roles in PADC, I learned that leadership is not about titles, but about making an impact. Managing members, organizing events, and improving communication taught me the value of teamwork, problem solving, and resilience. Every challenge helped me grow, and every success showed me the power of working together.

The Technical Awakening

I was introduced to programming through a required course, and it soon became a passion. I enjoyed the logic, creativity, and problem solving in coding. When I built my first interactive webpage, I realized that technology could help me make a bigger impact. I also saw how my leadership skills fit well with coding, such as understanding user needs, managing time, and working well with a team.

Bridging Two Worlds

Today, I combine leadership and technology. My experience in organizing helps me approach technical problems with empathy, planning, and a focus on real impact. My coding skills let me bring new solutions to leadership challenges. This mix is not just my strength, it is who I am. I am not choosing between being a leader or a developer; I am a leader-developer, and that is where I belong.
